Baseball Knowledge and Coaching Platform.

Sample Activity

Play. Evaluate. Compete.

What’s My Play is a one of a kind online, fun, dynamic, and interactive learning platform that allows Coaches, Players, and Organizations to track and assess their knowledge of live game situational play at any given point before, during or after a season. By providing organizations and teams the ability to create knowledge tests for players and coaches, they are better equipped to see and measure what their teams and players are learning.

What’s My Play allows players too:

  • Set up an individual account
  • Test their current situational knowledge
  • Test their positional responsibility knowledg
  • See where they rank among their peers
  • Track progress to see areas in which they may need to improve upon
  • Challenge other players and compete against them

What’s My Play allows coaches too:

  • Set up a Coach/Team account
  • Create and assign activities to an individual or entire team
  • Create their own content and activities based on their own individual coaching philosophies
  • Receive instant feedback about their team or individual player performance
  • Differentiate assessments based on player or team experience level
  • Test other coaches as well as themselves
  • See where they rank amongst other coaches and teams
